Grooming Tips:

  1. Regular Brushing: Brush your Shiba Inu's coat at least 2-3 times a week to remove loose fur and prevent matting.
  2. Bathing: Bathe your Shiba Inu occasionally using a gentle dog shampoo to keep their coat and skin healthy.
  3. Nail Trimming: Trim your Shiba Inu's nails every 2-4 weeks to prevent overgrowth and discomfort.
  4. Ear Cleaning: Clean your Shiba Inu's ears regularly to prevent wax buildup and potential infections.
  5. Dental Care: Brush your Shiba Inu's teeth regularly and provide dental chews or toys to maintain dental hygiene.
  6. Eye Care: Keep your Shiba Inu's eyes clean and free from discharge or tear stains.
  7. Grooming Professional: Consider visiting a professional groomer for occasional grooming sessions.
  8. Regular Checkups: Schedule regular veterinary checkups to monitor your Shiba Inu's overall health.
