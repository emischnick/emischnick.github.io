General Facts about Shiba Inus

History Grooming Home Gallery

Fact 1: Origin

Shiba Inus are a Japanese breed that dates back centuries. They were originally bred for hunting in the mountainous regions of Japan.

Fact 2: Size

Shiba Inus are small to medium-sized dogs, typically weighing between 17-23 pounds and standing about 13-17 inches tall at the shoulder.

Fact 3: Temperament

Shiba Inus are known for their spirited and independent nature. They are also loyal and affectionate with their families but can be aloof with strangers.

Fact 4: Coat

Shiba Inus have a double coat consisting of a soft undercoat and a straight, coarse outer coat. They shed heavily twice a year and require regular grooming.
